3.1 Applications to ARUs

ARUs often capture voice notes, but existing speech-to-text recognition methods, as well as human intervention to decode voice notes, have shown limited effectiveness or high resource consumption. Instead, employing DTMF allows efficient encryption of substantial data within a short time frame, rendering reproduction and decoding challenging for unauthorized parties.

3.2 Applications to remote cameras

In contrast to using whiteboards, QR codes offer superior error correction capabilities, ensuring reliable data transmission, especially in noisy environments or instances of poor image capture. Furthermore, QR codes enable quick and efficient scanning and decryption by authorized receivers, simplifying the overall decryption process.
